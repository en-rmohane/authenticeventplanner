Pricing FAQs

Our pricing varies by city to ensure fairness and value:

  • Metro Cities: Higher material costs, specialized teams, intricate designs
  • Tier 2/3 Cities: Good quality materials, experienced teams
  • Local Cities: Budget-friendly options without compromising beauty

You pay for the value appropriate to your location.

All our quotes include:

  • Complete decoration as per package
  • Material costs (as per selected grade)
  • Labor charges
  • Transportation within city
  • Setup and dismantling
  • Basic maintenance during event

Note: Venue rental, food, and photography are not included unless specified.

No hidden charges! We believe in complete transparency:

  • All costs are mentioned in the quote

If any additional services are requested during the event, they will be discussed and approved by you first.

Our standard payment terms are:

  • 50% advance to confirm booking
  • 40% 7 days before the event
  • 10% balance after event completion

For corporate clients and bulk bookings, we offer customized payment plans.
