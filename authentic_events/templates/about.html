The Legacy

Crafting Timeless Moments Since 2014

Authentic Event Planner was born out of a simple passion: to transform ordinary spaces into majestic, royal environments for life's most precious celebrations. Starting as a boutique floral decorator in Delhi, we have expanded to become a premier full-service planning agency recognized across India.

We believe that a wedding or milestone birthday is not just an event; it is an emotional landmark. That is why our decor features a signature blend of royal deep reds, glowing golds, and premium ivory setups that leave guests spellbound.
